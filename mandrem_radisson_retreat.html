Overview
A rare gem in Goa's hospitality landscape — Mandrem Beach Resort offers only 41 boutique rooms with the extraordinary privilege of dual frontage: both beachfront and riverfront access on the serene Mandrem coastline. Part of Radisson Individuals Retreats, this is the ideal choice for exclusive leadership retreats, intimate weddings, and full property buyouts where privacy and exclusivity are paramount.
