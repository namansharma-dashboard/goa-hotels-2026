Overview
Goa's newest luxury 5-star resort on the Vagator seafront, JW Marriott Goa redefines group hospitality on the Konkan coast. With 32,440 sq.ft of state-of-the-art event space, India's only Nikkei restaurant (Heliconia), a dramatic infinity pool overlooking the Arabian Sea, and Spa by L'Occitane — this is the flagship property for premium corporate groups, incentive travel, and destination weddings in North Goa.
