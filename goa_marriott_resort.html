Overview
The only 5-star hotel on Miramar Beach — Goa Marriott Resort & Spa sits at the confluence of the Mandovi River and the Arabian Sea, offering unmatched city and sea views. Its AP Plan (all meals included) makes it a hassle-free choice for corporate groups, while the on-site Casino Carnival (Goa's largest casino) and 14,144 sq.ft of meeting space make it ideal for incentive travel and high-energy MICE programs.
