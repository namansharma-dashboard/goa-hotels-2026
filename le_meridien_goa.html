Le Méridien Goa, Calangute
📍 Aguada Siolim Road, Calangute · 30 km Mopa · 5 min to beach
Overview
Marriott Bonvoy's 5-star offering in the heart of Calangute, Le Méridien Goa blends European sophistication with Goan warmth. With 1,200+ sqm of versatile indoor and outdoor event space, an open-air Telhado venue, and an inbuilt-stage Jazz & Grill hall, it's a top pick for mid-sized corporate groups, product launches, and social events seeking premium value.
